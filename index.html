Who We Are

A seasonal Caribbean restaurant, BK Bistro offers a variety of menu items to enjoy along with our BK-made wines. Our menu offers a selection of small plates, appetizers, and entrees that showcase fresh, seasonal ingredients sourced from local farmers and purveyors. Chef Benjamin Lambert takes inspiration from worldwide cuisine and presents fun, inventive dishes for guests to enjoy. We serve our Handcrafted Organically sourced ethnic wines from our Fig & Vine Urban Winery and offer a robust craft cocktail and beer program.

See all of our offerings here at BK Bistro | Fig & Vine Urban Winery. Visit us for Half Price Bottle night every Wednesday or for a special Date Night!

Sit down to a nice elegant Lunch or Dinner @ BK Bistro. Choose from a rotating selection of food and wine specials on offer daily in addition to our regular dinner menu.

Visit our neighboring Wine Bar for amazing small plates and our grand selection of fine wines. Come for a chat and sip, or a small tasting.

Check out our new private cabins for a great dining experience @ BK Bistro.
